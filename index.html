Why Git Er Done

Ease Of Use

You register online by entering your address, email and adding a credit card on file. You will receive a 'Git Er Done' laundry bag in the mail. Drop off laundry bag, within 48 hours pick up clean laundry and your credit card will be charged for service .

Cost Effective

All you need to do is drop off laundry bag and pick up clean clothes for the low cost of $1 a pound. No more wasting your time, buying detergent or dryer sheets.

Tracking

The Git Er Done platform will allow you to check-in your laundry when you drop it off. We will notify you upon pick up and a final time upon completion. This will allow you to know where your laundry is at all times.

Location

Drop off locations will be in apartment buildings, at dry cleaners and designated areas of Roanoke. Laundry becomes part of your morning commute to work, no more worrying about clean clothes.
